# Tattva Trails Explore

**Bridging Indian Art, Culture, and Tourism with Technology**

Tattva Trails Explore is a culturally immersive web platform developed to promote traditional Indian art forms, regional heritage, and responsible tourism using open data and interactive web technologies. Built during a hackathon under the theme *“Art, Culture, and Tourism”*, this project aims to reconnect modern audiences with India’s deep-rooted traditions through an engaging and data-driven experience.

## 🌍 Live Website

**[Explore Now](https://tattva-trails-explore.lovable.app/)**

---

## 📌 Features

- **Interactive Cultural Map** – Discover regional art forms, festivals, and cultural landmarks across Indian states.
- **Cultural Gallery** – View traditional Indian art styles like Madhubani, Warli, Pattachitra, and more.
- **Tourism Trends Integration** – Travel suggestions based on seasonal and regional data.
- **Responsible Travel Tips** – Encourages sustainable and ethical tourism.
- **Cultural Calendar** – Plan visits around traditional festivals and regional events.
